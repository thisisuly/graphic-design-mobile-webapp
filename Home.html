My Values


Communication:A designer's job is to communicate a client's story, brand and ideas. As such I aim to have excellent communication skills to present any work I should need to do for a client.

Openness:In graphic design, we have to be open-minded, and willing to try new things. I hope any client that I have can also be open and honest with myself with their opinions on the designs i present to and for them.

Patience:Design work can be slow going. The results shown on my page don't often come quickly so patience is a must to let an idea develop. Working on communication and trying out creative alternatives will be necessary before getting to the result

At the end of the day: Just go with the flow!
